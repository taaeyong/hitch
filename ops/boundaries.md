# Boundaries

## Private Data Boundary
The following must not be pushed to remote git:
- girlfriend raw txt data
- mother raw txt data
- private relationship wiki data
- private relationship summaries derived directly from sensitive real-world material
- raw simulation grounding evidence tied to real private conversations

These should remain local-only or otherwise be excluded from remote publication.

## Demo-Safe Push Boundary
The following may be pushed if sanitized:
- sanitized wiki snapshots
- genericized pattern or signal files
- graph payload samples
- sanitized weekly report samples
- ontology/schema examples
- product/spec/code/ops/test artifacts

## Product vs Data Distinction
Product building should continue through normal commit/push flow.
Private relationship data should remain outside the remote push boundary.
